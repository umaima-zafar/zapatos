Exchange Policy

Our Commitment to Your Satisfaction

At Zapatos, we understand that sometimes a product may not meet your expectations. If you need to exchange an item, please review our exchange policy below.

Eligibility for Exchange

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase to be eligible for an exchange.
  • The item must be in its original, unworn condition, with all tags and packaging intact.
  • Items marked as "final sale" cannot be exchanged.

How to Request an Exchange

  • To initiate an exchange, please contact our customer service team with your order number and details of the item you wish to exchange.
  • Once your exchange request is approved, we will provide instructions on how to return the item to us.

Processing Your Exchange

  • Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it to ensure it meets the exchange criteria.
  • If the item is eligible for exchange, we will process the exchange and ship the new item to you within 7 business days.
  • If the new item is of greater value, you will be required to pay the difference.

Shipping Costs

  • You are responsible for the shipping costs associated with returning the item for exchange.
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance, as we are not responsible for items lost in transit.

Damaged or Defective Items

  • If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a replacement item to be sent to you at no additional cost.
