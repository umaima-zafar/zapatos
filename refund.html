Refund Policy

Hassle-Free Returns

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from Zapatos. If you are not satisfied, our refund policy is designed to make the return process as easy as possible.

Eligibility for Refund

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase to be eligible for a refund.
  • The item must be in its original, unworn condition, with all tags and packaging intact.
  • Items marked as "final sale" are not eligible for a refund.

How to Request a Refund

  • To request a refund, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for the return.
  • Once your refund request is approved, we will provide instructions on how to return the item to us.

Processing Your Refund

  • Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it to ensure it meets the refund criteria.
  • If the item is eligible for a refund, we will process the refund to your original payment method within 7 business days.
  • Please note that it may take additional time for the refund to appear on your account, depending on your bank or credit card company.

Refund Exclusions

  • Shipping charges are non-refundable.
  • If you received a free gift with your purchase, the gift must be returned along with the item to receive a full refund. If the gift is not returned, the value of the gift will be deducted from the refund amount.

Returning Damaged or Defective Items

  • If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a return and issue a full refund or send a replacement at no additional cost.
